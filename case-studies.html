OwnerDirect.com

Reviving a 40-year vacation rental brand through AI-driven acquisition and AI-powered scale.

OwnerDirect.com is one of North America's longest-standing whole-home vacation rental brands. TravelAI acquired the domain and trademarks to strengthen North American traffic acquisition and meet partner demand for high-intent vacation rental guests. The objective was to modernize the brand using AI-powered infrastructure, automate content and SEO at scale, and convert historical brand equity into profitable organic demand.
