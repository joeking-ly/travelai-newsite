How AI Helped Me Plan the Perfect Family Road Trip

Planning a road trip with two kids under 5 seemed impossible. Then I discovered how TravelAI could customize recommendations for our family.

DP
David Park
Published Feb 12, 2026 • 7 min read

Two kids under five, a week on the road, and a fear of overplanned days that collapse by naptime. That was the brief. TravelAI suggested stops with playgrounds, short drives between bases, and family-friendly eats — and it actually matched our pace.

Stops That Actually Work for Kids

Instead of generic "family-friendly" lists, the recommendations considered our route, our kids' ages, and our preference for outdoor time. We found rest areas with green space, small towns with ice cream and short walks, and one unforgettable farm stay that the app suggested because we'd enjoyed similar stays before.

The Takeaway

A family road trip doesn't have to mean compromise. With AI that gets what you need — pit stops, flexibility, and a few guaranteed wins for the back seat — we had a week that felt like a real vacation, not just a transfer between naptimes.

The Verdict

AI didn't replace parental judgment — it gave us a shortlist of options that actually fit our family. Less scrolling, more driving. We'll do it again.
