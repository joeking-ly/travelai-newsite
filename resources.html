Frequently asked questions

What is TravelAI? +
TravelAI is a global leader in applied artificial intelligence for travel. We build a personalization ecosystem that helps travelers discover and book the right trips across 470+ consumer-facing brands, powered by privacy-first AI like the Traveler Action Model (TAM) and the Shared Vector Travel Protocol (SVTP).
How does the Traveler Action Model (TAM) work? +
The TAM is a compact, pseudonymous representation of a traveler’s preferences—built from behavior and outcomes without storing personally identifiable information. It learns over time so recommendations get better across our network while keeping data portable and under the traveler’s control.
What is the Shared Vector Travel Protocol (SVTP)? +
SVTP is our consent-based protocol for sharing traveler context between partners and AI agents. It allows warm handoffs with full personalization so the next site or agent can serve the traveler well from the first interaction, without ever seeing raw PII.
How do I partner with TravelAI? +
We work with travel brands, OTAs, accommodation providers, and technology partners. Visit our Partners page to learn about integration options, or reach out via Contact to start a conversation.
What makes TravelAI different from other travel platforms? +
We focus on privacy-first personalization at network scale: one learning system across 19.5M+ listings and 470+ brands, with portable traveler context (TAM + SVTP) so personalization follows the traveler instead of locking them into a single walled garden.
Is TravelAI hiring? +
Yes. We’re always looking for talented people in engineering, product, growth, and operations. Check our Contact page for open roles or get in touch to learn more.
