TravelAI Acquires OwnerDirect.com, Strengthening Its North American Vacation Rental Presence

Owner Direct Vacation Rentals brings nearly 40 years of trusted whole-home booking to the TravelAI ecosystem.

TA
TravelAI Team
Published Dec 10, 2025 • 5 min read

VANCOUVER, BC – DECEMBER 10, 2025 – TravelAI, a global leader in applied artificial intelligence for travel, today announced the acquisition of the domain and trademarks for OwnerDirect.com, one of North America's longest-standing whole-home vacation rental brands. The acquisition includes all associated intellectual property, trademarks, and domain names.

First established in 1987, Owner Direct Vacation Rentals has served millions of travelers looking for reliable, great-value whole-home vacation rentals across Canada, the United States, and beyond. With this latest acquisition, TravelAI aims to expand its reach in North American traffic acquisition.

Honoring a Vacation Rental Legacy That Began in 1987

Owner Direct's roots trace back to the early days of online vacation rentals, with commercial use of the OwnerDirect.com domain dating back to 1994. "Owner Direct has been part of the North American travel fabric for decades," said John Lyotier, CEO and Co-founder. "We are proud to carry that story forward and amplify it globally using AI."

TravelAI's Growth Strategy: Innovation + Acquisition

The acquisition reflects TravelAI's proven approach of growing through deep category expertise, AI-powered marketing infrastructure, and strategic brand acquisitions. TravelAI's network now includes more than 475 consumer-facing brands, forming the backbone of its vision for "The Web of One"—a hyper-personalized travel experience where every traveler's journey is uniquely matched by AI.

Driving North American Traveler Demand

Owner Direct becomes a key brand in TravelAI's North American growth strategy. TravelAI plans to position Owner Direct Vacation Rentals as a flagship in its short-term rental portfolio, re-engage historical audiences, scale SEO and performance marketing, and modernize the OwnerDirect.com experience with AI personalization.

Part of TravelAI's Expansive Ecosystem

OwnerDirect.com joins TravelAI's family of travel brands, including VacationCottage.com, SkiHotels.com, BedroomVillas.com, Casai.com, Hotala.com, RentByOwner.com, and others—collectively forming TravelAI's AI-driven network.

The Bottom Line

OwnerDirect.com gives TravelAI a legacy platform with deep credibility in North America. Combined with the applied AI stack, TravelAI can deliver more value to partners, better matches for travelers, and a significantly expanded footprint across Canada and the U.S.
