OwnerDirect.com

Reviving a 40-year vacation rental brand through AI-driven acquisition.

Background

OwnerDirect.com is one of North America's longest-standing whole-home vacation rental brands, first established in 1987 and commercially active online since the early days of the internet. Over nearly four decades, the brand earned a reputation for trust, value, and owner-direct vacation rental discovery across Canada and the United States.

Strategic Rationale

TravelAI acquired the OwnerDirect.com domain, trademarks, and associated intellectual property as part of its strategy to strengthen North American traffic acquisition and meet growing partner demand for high-intent vacation rental guests. Partners had consistently identified North America as a priority growth market, and OwnerDirect's legacy domain authority, historical trust signals, and brand recognition made it a uniquely attractive acquisition.

By bringing OwnerDirect into the TravelAI ecosystem, the objective was not simply to relaunch a legacy site, but to modernize it using AI-powered infrastructure, automate content and SEO at scale, and rapidly convert historical brand equity into profitable organic demand.

Asset Turnaround

CASAI.com

Resurrecting a $50M brand from a $30k entry price.

Background

The previous owner (another travel company) had raised nearly $50M in cash and debt and spent aggressively on brand building without achieving profitability, before closing in 2023. This meant a good external link profile existed, and all TravelAI needed to do was to quickly deploy a live site and create content at scale.

Strategic Rationale

TravelAI was looking for a good asset around which it could build a new, luxury short-term rental brand, particularly for the Caribbean and Mediterranean markets as requested by its partners who wanted more growth from these markets.

It needed a brandable concept to take to international audiences. The domain, Casai.com, came up for auction, and the company pounced. TravelAI purchased the domain for $30,000.
