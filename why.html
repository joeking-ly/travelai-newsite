From startup to AI travel leader

A story of compounding growth, continuous learning, and unwavering focus on making travel better for everyone.

2018

Foundation

Founded with a simple mission: use technology to help travelers find their perfect accommodations. Started with a single vacation rental brand and a vision for AI-powered personalization.

2020

Multi-Brand Strategy

Launched the multi-brand approach, creating niche travel sites targeted to specific traveler personas and destinations. Began building the data foundation that would power future AI capabilities.

2022

AI Transformation

Pivoted to AI-first operations. Began using generative AI for content creation and ML for traffic optimization. Revenue accelerated from ~$2M to double-digit millions. The self-improving flywheel took off.

2024

Agentic Architecture

Launched first agentic bots across the network. Deployed Presto for autonomous website generation. Acquired and relaunched CASAI ($45M GBV in 20 months) and OwnerDirect (payback in 2 months). Expanded to 470+ brands.

2025

Vectorization & Scale

Achieved $456M GBV, 60% YoY growth, and $6.1M EBITDA. Began development of the Shared Vector Travel Protocol (SVTP) and Traveler Action Models (TAMs). Positioned for the agentic age.

2026+

The Agentic Network

Building the context layer for travel — a privacy-first personalization ecosystem that spans brands, partners, and AI agents. Target: one billion travelers by 2030. The future of travel is agentic, and we're defining it.
