TravelAI Acquires Smartours.com, Launches Push into AI-Powered Packaged Travel

TravelAI expands its ecosystem with the acquisition of Smartours.com, combining AI personalization with tours, activities, and packaged travel experiences.

TA
TravelAI Team
Published Jan 10, 2026 • 5 min read

VANCOUVER, BC – JANUARY 10, 2026 – TravelAI, a global leader in applied artificial intelligence for travel, today announced the acquisition of Smartours.com, marking a strategic push into AI-powered packaged travel. The acquisition brings tours, activities, and experiential travel into the TravelAI network, enabling end-to-end trip planning and booking across accommodations and experiences.

Smartours has long served travelers seeking curated tours and packaged experiences. By integrating Smartours into the TravelAI ecosystem, the company can apply its personalization and discovery technology to the full journey—from where you stay to what you do when you get there.

Why Packaged Travel Matters for AI

Packaged travel—tours, activities, multi-day experiences—represents a growing share of how travelers plan and book. AI that understands preferences and context can match travelers with the right experiences at the right time, reducing friction and improving satisfaction. This acquisition positions TravelAI to offer a more complete, AI-driven travel experience across its 470+ brand network.

One Network, End-to-End Journeys

TravelAI's vision of "The Web of One" centers on hyper-personalized travel where every touchpoint is informed by a unified understanding of the traveler. Adding Smartours.com extends that capability into tours and activities, so recommendations can span lodging, transportation, and experiences in a single, coherent flow.

The Bottom Line

The Smartours acquisition signals TravelAI's commitment to powering the full trip lifecycle with AI. As the network grows, travelers get more relevant options and partners get a broader, smarter distribution channel for packaged travel.
